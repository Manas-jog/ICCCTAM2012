Conference Overview

The next big thing to happen for the software industry after the dot com boom, Cloud Computing is redefining the way we think about software and the way it is used. A very simple yet complex technology, Cloud Computing is constantly evolving to provide simpler and easy solutions to end consumers and even business enterprises. The basic idea behind the concept is to provide applications, software and a host of other services to the end user through a network. The proliferation in the cloud deployment has been made possible by the advances in virtualization technology, networking technology and storage technology. One of the major advantages of this model is that it allows customers and big companies the joy of hassle free computing and data management while keeping the costs low at the same time.


International Conference on Cloud Computing Technologies, Applications and Management 2012 is a coveted technically driven premier event which also provides a platform for academicians, researchers, practitioners and users to share and discuss the scope and challenges faces in cloud computing. Not only is ICCCTAM is one of the first international conferences on Cloud computing to be held in this region, it has received an overwhelming response from the students and researchers who would be presenting their papers with an estimated guest list of around 200 delegates expected to grace the event.

Keynote Speakers

  • Dr. Eesa M. Bastaki

    CEO, ICT Fund, Dubai, UAE

    Dr. Eesa Mohammed Bastaki, Ph.D. is the CEO of the ICT foundation and is actively leading the effort to create an eco-system in the UAE ICT industry by encouraging entrepreneurship, funding R&D projects and University scholarships and implementing ICT initiatives at the school level.
    As a UAE national scientist and researcher, he completed his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Electrical Engineering from University of California, Irvine. His research interest are Multiple Access Communications, Coding and Synchronizing. He is the recipient for Sheikh Rashid’s Award for Scientific Excellence. In 2009, he received UAE’s highest award “The Emirates Award for Science, Art and Literature” in Sciences.

  • Prof. Dr. Peter Varman

    Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Rice University, Houston, TX, USA

    Prof. Dr. Peter J. Varman, a scholar in Residence at VMware, currently is a professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rice University. His skills and expertise lies in the field of Virtualization, Cloud Computing and Resource Management. Previously, he had a work experience as a Program Director at Computer Systems Architecture at National Science Foundation and also as Regional Sales Manager at IBM Research Lab. He completed his B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from IIT-Kanpur and Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from University of Texas at Austin. Professor Peter Varman’s research interests are parallel algorithms and architectures, parallel I/O and the performance of database systems. His research deals with design, analysis and performance evaluation of parallel algorithm with special emphasis on non-numerical problems like sorting and searching, graph theory and computational geometry.

  • Dr. Ahmed Al Dabbagh

    Technology Development Manager, Ankabut, UAE

    A pioneer in the field of e-learning platform and resources, Dr. Ahmed Dabbagh has a vast experience of 18 years in senior technical and academic positions. Currently working as Manager – Technology and Services Development at Ankabut UAE Advance network for research and Education, he is also an Adjunct faculty member at the Hamdan Bin Mohammed e-University, teaching post Graduate courses in educational technologies. Previously as a member of Ajman University Of Science and Technology, he established the e-services Department at AUST to begin a new era in e-Educational Learning.
    Apart from being the Co-author of 2 books namely- “Intellectual Property for Electronic Systems: An essential introduction”, IEC2007 and Cognitively Informed Intelligent Interfaces: Systems Design and Development he is also the holder of 6 International patents(3 European,2 American and one in Japan).
    Dr Ahmed Dabbagh has been actively associated with IEEE, IEEE-Computer Society, VSI and various other organizations. Besides e-learning his other areas of specialization includes microelectronics, telecommunications and many more.

  • Dr. Osman Ahmed

    VP and Head, Centre of Excellence for Smart Buildings at Masdar City, Siemens, Abu Dhabi, UAE

    Considered one of the most successful innovators in the field of micro electromechanical systems(MEMS),Dr. Osman Ahmed has been working with Siemens for the past 25 years and is presently the VP and Head, Centre of Excellence for Smart Buildings at Masdar City, Siemens, Abu Dhabi, UAE. Having studied in Bangladesh,Canada and the US he always wanted to do something different as a person. It was in 1997 that he first heard about Micro Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS) at a conference and this went on to become the focus of his career and since then there has been no looking back. A holder of 75 patents, Dr. Osman is an innovator and techno-prenuer who is focused on emerging technologies for the creation of corporate assets- both intellectual and capital.
    His research focus and work covers a wide variety of topics such as applications of artificial intelligence in building systems to model based virtual building systems for commissioning, 3D visualization, and advanced applications. Currently engaged in establishing an open innovation network model around the globe, he is also a two-time technical achievement award winner within the SBT as well as the recipient of 2004 prestigious “Siemens Inventor of the Year ” award for his work on micro-system application for buildings.

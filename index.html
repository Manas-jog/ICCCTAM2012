Conference Overview

The next big thing to happen for the software industry after the dot com boom, Cloud Computing is redefining the way we think about software and the way it is used. A very simple yet complex technology, Cloud Computing is constantly evolving to provide simpler and easy solutions to end consumers and even business enterprises. The basic idea behind the concept is to provide applications, software and a host of other services to the end user through a network. The proliferation in the cloud deployment has been made possible by the advances in virtualization technology, networking technology and storage technology. One of the major advantages of this model is that it allows customers and big companies the joy of hassle free computing and data management while keeping the costs low at the same time.


International Conference on Cloud Computing Technologies, Applications and Management 2012 is a coveted technically driven premier event which also provides a platform for academicians, researchers, practitioners and users to share and discuss the scope and challenges faces in cloud computing. Not only is ICCCTAM is one of the first international conferences on Cloud computing to be held in this region, it has received an overwhelming response from the students and researchers who would be presenting their papers with an estimated guest list of around 200 delegates expected to grace the event.


The conference focuses on, but is not limited to the following three thrust areas:

  • Technologies

    This area will focus on the technologies being used. Topics include Cloud Architecture, Virtualization & Scalability and Cloud Security & Exploits.

  • Applications

    This area will cover topics that leverage the Cloud to address contemporary problems. Topics include Crowd Sourcing, Bioinformatics and e-Governance.

  • Management

    This area will include topics related to the overall management of a cloud service like Business Models, Data Security and Risk Management.

Industry Track will bring together the practitioners from the cloud computing industry both solution providers and solution seekers. They would show case their exhibits, experiences, challenges and success, enabling them to attract prospective customers seeking cloud computing solutions.

Students’ Poster Session is to recognize excellence in projects relating to cloud computing and applications.

Keynote Speakers

  • Dr. Eesa M. Bastaki

    CEO, ICT Fund, Dubai, UAE

    Dr. Eesa Mohammed Bastaki, Ph.D. is the CEO of the ICT foundation and is actively leading the effort to create an eco-system in the UAE ICT industry by encouraging entrepreneurship, funding R&D projects and University scholarships and implementing ICT initiatives at the school level.

    As a UAE national scientist and researcher, he completed his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Electrical Engineering from University of California, Irvine. His research interest are Multiple Access Communications, Coding and Synchronizing. He is the recipient for Sheikh Rashid’s Award for Scientific Excellence. In 2009, he received UAE’s highest award “The Emirates Award for Science, Art and Literature” in Sciences.

  • Prof. Dr. Peter Varman

    Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Rice University, Houston, TX, USA

    Prof. Dr. Peter J. Varman, a scholar in Residence at VMware, currently is a professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rice University. His skills and expertise lies in the field of Virtualization, Cloud Computing and Resource Management. Previously, he had a work experience as a Program Director at Computer Systems Architecture at National Science Foundation and also as Regional Sales Manager at IBM Research Lab. He completed his B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from IIT-Kanpur and Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from University of Texas at Austin.


    Professor Peter Varman’s research interests are parallel algorithms and architectures, parallel I/O and the performance of database systems. His research deals with design, analysis and performance evaluation of parallel algorithm with special emphasis on non-numerical problems like sorting and searching, graph theory and computational geometry.

  • Dr. Ahmed Al Dabbagh

    Technology Development Manager, Ankabut, UAE

    A pioneer in the field of e-learning platform and resources, Dr. Ahmed Dabbagh has a vast experience of 18 years in senior technical and academic positions. Currently working as Manager of Technology and Services Development at Ankabut UAE Advance network for research and Education, he is also an adjunct faculty member at the Hamdan Bin Mohammed e-University, teaching post-graduate courses in educational technologies. Previously, as a member of Ajman University of Science and Technology, he established the e-services Department at AUST to begin a new era in e-Educational Learning.

    Apart from being the Co-author of 2 books namely: Intellectual Property for Electronic Systems: An essential introduction, IEC2007 and Cognitively Informed Intelligent Interfaces: Systems Design and Development, he is also the holder of 6 International patents (3 European, 2 American and one in Japan). Dr Ahmed Dabbagh has been actively associated with IEEE's Computer Society, VSI and various other organizations. Besides e-learning his other areas of specialization includes microelectronics, telecommunications and many more.

  • Dr. Osman Ahmed

    VP and Head, Centre of Excellence for Smart Buildings at Masdar City, Siemens, Abu Dhabi, UAE

    Considered one of the most successful innovators in the field of Micro Electromechanical Systems (MEMS), Dr. Osman Ahmed has been working with Siemens for the past 25 years and is presently the VP and Head, Centre of Excellence for Smart Buildings at Masdar City, Siemens, Abu Dhabi, UAE. Having studied in Bangladesh, Canada and the US, he always wanted to do something different as a person. It was in 1997 that he first heard about MEMS at a conference and this went on to become the focus of his career and since then there has been no looking back.

    A holder of 75 patents, Dr. Osman is an innovator and techno-prenuer who is focused on emerging technologies for the creation of corporate assets- both intellectual and capital. His research focus and work covers a wide variety of topics such as applications of artificial intelligence in building systems to model-based virtual building systems for commissioning, 3D visualization, and advanced applications. Currently engaged in establishing an open innovation network model around the globe, he is also a two-time technical achievement award winner within the SBT as well as the recipient of 2004 prestigious Siemens Inventor of the Year award for his work on micro-system application for buildings.

Getting There


Transportation

Situated away from the din of the main city, Bits Pilani Dubai is one of the most happening colleges in Dubai International Academic City (DIAC). Private vehicles or taking a direct taxi are the fastest way to reach the campus by following the map. Alternatively, one can get to the campus using the convenient and highly effective public transport services of Dubai.


Follow these guidelines to reach BITS Pilani, Dubai campus via public transport:

  • Take a metro to Al Rashidiya on the red line from the metro station closest to your current location.
  • From Al Rashidiya you can hire a cab to BITS Pilani, Academic City or board the bus numbered 365 to International City and get down at BITS Pilani 1.

Sightseeing

Dubai as the world knows today, is a global city and a business hub. The city of Dubai is an emirate within the United Arab Emirates (UAE) located south east of the Persian Gulf on the Arabian Peninsula. Dubai is one of the only two emirates to have veto power over critical matters of national importance in the country’s political matters. Dubai, the cosmopolitan city is a venue for various globally acclaimed events. Its unique attraction is that it brings together the highest standard of comforts and convenience with mystical adventure of Arabia. With an amazing combination of stunning skyscrapers to the majestic deserts, Dubai provides a truly amazing destination for corporate gatherings and tourism activities. Offering all this and more, Dubai has emerged as a global destination of premier meetings and conferences and is regarded as one of the best locations in the world.


Here are the list of places that you should visit during your stay in Dubai :

  • Burj Khalifa

    This stunning sky scrapper is symbolic of the achievements and pinnacles of success reached by mankind. This tallest tower of the world is the heart of Dubai and you would regret if you miss out this place during your visit to the amazing city of Dubai.
